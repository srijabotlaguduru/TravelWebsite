OVER VIEW

Infrastructure: Infrastructure: Singapore is renowned for its modern infrastructure and efficient public services. The city-state has an extensive transportation network, including an advanced subway system, buses, and taxis. It also has a world-class airport, Changi Airport, which is consistently ranked among the best airports globally.

Culture: Singapore has a rich cultural heritage influenced by its diverse population. The country celebrates various festivals, such as Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa (Eid al-Fitr), Deepavali (Diwali), and Christmas. English, Malay, Mandarin Chinese, and Tamil are the official languages, reflecting the multicultural nature of the society.

Culture: Despite its small size, Singapore attracts a significant number of tourists each year. Visitors come to experience its vibrant cityscape, iconic landmarks like Marina Bay Sands and Gardens by the Bay, diverse culinary scene, and cultural attractions like Chinatown and Little India. The country is also known for its strict cleanliness and low crime rate.
